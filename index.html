Common questions about our service

Saju is Korea's answer to astrology — but instead of star signs, it's based on the exact year, month, day, and hour you were born. Think of it as your cosmic DNA. Each birth moment creates a unique energy blueprint that reveals your personality, love patterns, career strengths, and what's coming next. Koreans have trusted this system for over 1,000 years — now AI makes it accessible to everyone.
A Compatibility Reading compares two people's Saju charts to reveal your relationship dynamics — chemistry, conflict points, and how to make it work. Whether it's a crush, partner, or even a friend, it shows what's really going on between you two.
Saju is all about YOU — your personality, career path, love life, and what this year holds. Compatibility is about the TWO of you — what pulls you together, where you'll clash, and whether it's built to last.
Western astrology groups you by birth month into 12 star signs. Saju uses all four pillars — year, month, day, AND hour — creating a far more detailed and personal reading unique to you. No two Saju charts are alike.
We use the same traditional calculations that professional Korean Saju masters have relied on for centuries — including True Solar Time correction for your exact birth location. That said, think of it as a mirror, not a crystal ball. It's designed for self-reflection and entertainment, not predicting lottery numbers.
